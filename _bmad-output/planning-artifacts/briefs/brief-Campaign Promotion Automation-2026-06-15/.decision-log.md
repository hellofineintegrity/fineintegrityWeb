---

### 2026-06-15 — Product name confirmed: Prachar (प्रचार)

**Decision:** Product name is **Prachar (प्रचार)** — Hindi/Sanskrit for "promotion" or "publicity."

**Options considered:** PromoCraft, Prachar, FestivAI, CampaignSpark, BrandReach  
**Chosen by:** Nilesh  
**Rationale:** Authentic to the Indian market; memorable; resonates with the Tier 2 city audience the product serves.

---

### 2026-06-15 — Website copy language: English only for v1

**Decision:** v1 marketing website copy is English only. AI-generated campaign content within the product supports Hindi, but the website itself does not. Hindi website version deferred to post-pilot.

**Rationale:** Consistent with PRD non-goal "Multi-language Platform UI — Not in v1."

---

### 2026-06-15 — Pricing transparency: all tiers visible, no "Contact Sales"

**Decision:** All pricing tiers (₹0 / ₹999 / ₹1,999 / ₹3,499) are displayed openly on the website without requiring a form fill or sales contact.

**Rationale:** Indian SMB buyers require visible pricing to engage. Gating pricing behind "Contact Sales" increases bounce rate for this audience.

---

### 2026-06-15 — Social proof: pilot businesses by reference only (with permission)

**Decision:** Pilot business names (AutoCare Services, Shree Ladies Garments, Fresh Basket) referenced only with explicit permission. If unavailable at launch, website uses industry category examples instead.

**Rationale:** Pilot is pre-launch; businesses have not consented to public endorsement yet.
