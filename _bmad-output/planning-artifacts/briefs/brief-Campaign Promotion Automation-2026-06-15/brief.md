# Product Brief: Marketing Website for Prachar by Fine Integrity

## Executive Summary

Indian small and mid-sized businesses lose sales every festival season because their promotional outreach is slow, manual, and unprofessional — WhatsApp messages typed by hand, sent without approval, tracked by nobody. Prachar, Fine Integrity's AI-powered campaign automation SaaS, solves this end-to-end: describe a promotion in plain English or Hindi, get a professionally designed campaign in seconds, route it through an approval workflow, and publish to WhatsApp and email — all in one platform.

The marketing website at fineintegrity.com is the primary acquisition channel. Its one job: convert a skeptical Indian SMB owner or admin into a free trial sign-up without requiring a sales call. The site must translate platform capability into concrete, festival-season outcomes ("Send a Diwali offer to your WhatsApp customers in 5 minutes — no designer needed") and remove the two largest barriers to sign-up: confusion about what the product does and distrust of new software.

## The Problem the Website Must Solve

The product solves a real business problem. The website has three conversion hurdles to clear before a visitor signs up:

**Comprehension.** "AI-powered campaign automation" means nothing to a garments shop owner in Pune. The website must translate platform capability into immediate, recognizable outcomes: professional Diwali promotions, WhatsApp campaigns that look like the national brands, knowing which offers drove footfall.

**Trust.** Indian SMBs are cautious about new software. They've been burned by tools that promise simplicity and deliver a support ticket queue. The website must signal legitimacy through professional design, real pilot business references, transparent INR pricing, and zero-risk entry (1-month free trial, no payment required).

**Urgency.** Promotional value is seasonal. A garments owner who misses Navratri can't recover that window. The website must connect platform value to the specific calendar moments — Navratri, Diwali, Eid, monsoon — the visitor already has circled.

## Who This Serves

**Primary — Business Admin or Owner:** Runs a retail, food, automotive, or services SMB in India. Currently promotes via manual WhatsApp broadcasts or printed flyers. Makes or influences software purchasing decisions; budget-conscious. Browses on Android mobile in a bilingual English + Hindi mental model. Skeptical of B2B SaaS promises; responds to specific outcomes and peer proof.

**Secondary — Business Executive:** Discovers Prachar via referral or organic search, wants to show the boss a tool that saves an hour per promotion. Will share the URL before a decision is made — the website must work as a forwarded link, not just a search landing page.

**Not the target for v1:** Enterprise marketing teams, non-Indian businesses, developer audiences.

## What the Website Communicates

fineintegrity.com is a conversion-focused site structured to walk a first-time visitor from awareness to free trial sign-up in a single scroll on mobile. It is not a help centre, a blog, or a feature encyclopedia.

**Core messages, in priority order:**
1. Describe any promotion in plain English or Hindi — AI generates a professional, graphics-rich campaign in seconds.
2. Send via WhatsApp and email from one platform, to opted-in customers.
3. Built for Indian festivals, Indian businesses, Indian pricing (₹999/month to start).
4. Free for 1 month — no credit card, no salesperson, 5 minutes to your first campaign.

Three things genuinely differentiate Prachar from the alternatives (Zoko, Interakt, WATI, manual WhatsApp Business broadcasts). The website must communicate these specifically, not as a generic feature list:

**1. AI that writes for Indian festivals and in Indian languages.** Not just a message scheduler — describe "Diwali 30% off all sarees, valid till October 30" and receive a designed, culturally relevant WhatsApp template in under 15 seconds, in English or Hindi, with brand logo and colors already applied. Competitors require manual template creation.

**2. Both channels, one dashboard.** WhatsApp and email analytics in a single campaign view — delivery, read rate, click-through, opt-outs. No stitching together two separate tools and two separate reports.

**3. Priced for Indian SMB reality.** Starting at ₹999/month per branch — a price point a 3-branch automotive services chain or a garments shop can approve without a finance committee. The free tier gives 1 month and 5 full campaigns to prove value before spending a rupee.

The website should not claim AI moats or proprietary technology. Execution speed and Indian-market specificity are the honest advantages at this stage.

## Success Criteria

All metrics are tracked from day one of launch. Do not optimize for time-on-site or page views at the expense of sign-up conversion — a visitor who reads five pages and does not sign up is a missed conversion, not an engaged user.

| Signal | Target | Measurement |
|---|---|---|
| Free trial sign-up conversion rate | ≥3% of unique visitors | Account creation events |
| Median time-to-sign-up from landing | ≤90 seconds | Session analytics |
| Mobile (Android) bounce rate | ≤50% | Device-segmented analytics |
| Pricing section click-through from homepage | ≥40% of homepage sessions | Scroll / click analytics |
| Indian Tier 1+2 city traffic share | ≥80% of sign-ups | Geolocation in analytics |

## Scope

### In Scope — v1 (Launching with October 2026 Pilot)

- **Homepage / hero:** One-line value proposition, primary CTA (Start Free Trial — No Credit Card Required), and 3-step how-it-works visual (Describe → AI Generates → Approve & Send)
- **Features section:** AI campaign generation, Indian Festival Calendar, WhatsApp + Email delivery, approval workflow, performance analytics — described in outcome language, not technical language
- **Pricing section:** All four tiers (Free Trial / Starter ₹999 / Growth ₹1,999 / Business ₹3,499) with included message volumes, overage rates, and free trial CTA on each tier
- **Social proof:** 1–2 pilot business references (with permission) or industry category examples if references are unavailable at launch
- **Sign-up / free trial entry:** Integrated with product registration (Gmail OAuth / email / mobile OTP)
- **Contact link:** Email or contact form; no live chat in v1
- **Mobile-responsive design:** All sections fully operable on a 375px viewport (Android); primary design target is mobile-first
- **Basic SEO:** Title tags, meta descriptions, Open Graph tags, structured data for business software category
- **Legal baseline:** Privacy policy and terms of service pages (required before public launch)

### Explicitly Out of Scope — v1

Blog, help documentation, video demos, pilot case studies with real performance numbers, Hindi language version of the website (v1 copy is English; AI-generated campaign content supports Hindi), live chat, cookie consent beyond the bare minimum, A/B tested landing pages, and industry-specific landing pages.

Post-pilot growth adds industry landing pages, regional language versions, live case studies, self-serve onboarding integration, and a public roadmap.
