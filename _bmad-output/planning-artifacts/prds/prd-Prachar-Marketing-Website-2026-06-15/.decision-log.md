# Decision Log — Prachar Marketing Website PRD

**Project:** Campaign Promotion Automation  
**Artifact:** PRD — Prachar Marketing Website (fineintegrity.com)  
**Owner:** Nilesh (Fine Integrity)  
**Started:** 2026-06-15

---

## Decisions

### 2026-06-15 — v1 build: pure static, no CMS

**Decision:** v1 marketing website is a pure static build (HTML/CSS/JS). No headless CMS in v1.  
**Chosen by:** Nilesh  
**Rationale:** Simpler build and hosting for the pilot launch window. CMS migration planned post-launch.  
**How to apply:** All content lives in code. Pricing, copy, and testimonial updates require a code edit and redeploy. CMS migration is the highest-priority post-launch infrastructure upgrade for the marketing site.

---

### 2026-06-15 — Sign-up flow: separate subdomain (app.fineintegrity.com/register)

**Decision:** All "Start Free Trial" CTAs on the marketing site redirect to app.fineintegrity.com/register. Registration does not happen on fineintegrity.com.  
**Chosen by:** Nilesh  
**Rationale:** Product and marketing site are cleanly separated at the subdomain boundary. businessname.fineintegrity.com subdomains are the product's per-tenant pattern; app. is the shared registration entry point.  
**How to apply:** No forms or embedded sign-up flows on the marketing site. GA4 cross-domain measurement must link fineintegrity.com and app.fineintegrity.com (Open Question §8.3).

---

### 2026-06-15 — Analytics platform: Google Analytics 4

**Decision:** GA4 is the v1 analytics platform for the marketing site.  
**Rationale:** Free, widely supported, standard for Indian market audience measurement. PDPB data residency implications for analytics to be reviewed before scaling beyond India.  
**Status:** Assumed; confirm before implementation.

---

### 2026-06-15 — Hosting platform: Azure Static Web Apps, Central India region

**Decision:** Azure Static Web Apps (Central India / Pune region) is the hosting platform.  
**Rationale:** Aligns with Nilesh's deep Azure expertise; same region as the Prachar product infrastructure; lowest latency for Indian visitors.  
**Status:** Assumed; confirm before infrastructure setup.

---

### 2026-06-15 — Highlighted pricing tier: Growth (₹1,999)

**Decision:** Growth (₹1,999/month) is the "Most Popular" highlighted tier in the pricing table. Starter and Business carry no badge.  
**Chosen by:** Nilesh  
**Rationale:** Anchors visitors toward the mid-tier plan value rather than the entry-level price.

---

### 2026-06-15 — Social proof: pilot business names require explicit written permission

**Decision:** No pilot business is named on the marketing site without explicit written permission before launch. If no permission obtained, section uses industry/city callout.  
**Rationale:** Pilot businesses have not consented to public endorsement; naming them without consent creates a trust and legal risk.

---

### 2026-06-15 — Contact: email link only, no /contact page in v1

**Decision:** Contact is an email link (hello@fineintegrity.com) in header nav and footer. No standalone /contact page.  
**Rationale:** Pre-sales enquiry volume at pilot scale does not justify a contact form. Revisit after pilot.
